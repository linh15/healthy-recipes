Summer Roll

Description

This dish is the best for summer. You custom your own rolls using the ingredients on the table.
Great dish for family gathering where we make our food at the table while talking with each other and having a great time :)

Ingredients

Can be whatever of your choice as along as they compose these main elements: vegetables, protein, wrapping paper, sauce and (optional) carbs.
Below is the suggested list of ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Herbs (cilantro, mint, fish mint, etc.)
  • Carrot
  • Pineapple
  • Cucumber
  • Mushroom
  • Onion
  • Chicken breast
  • Tofu
  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Prepare veggie: wash lettuce and herbs; chop carrot, cucumber and pineapple into thin long strips; wash and slice mushroom and onion
  2. Cook veggi: Stir fry mushroom with onion
  3. Prepare protein: slice chicken breast into thin strips; cut tofu into long rectangle pieces
  4. Cook protein: stir fry chicken, air fry tofu
  5. Make the sauce: mix hoisin sauce, peanut butter and water then microwave for 4 minutes
  6. Final step: assemble!
