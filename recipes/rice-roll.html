Rice Roll

Description

Best breakfast!!!

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Ground pork
  • Dried mushroom
  • Shallot
  • Fish sauce
  • Lemon
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix rice flour with water
  2. Stir fry pork with shallot and mushroom to make the filling
  3. Make thin rice pancakes in the pan
  4. Take rice pancakes out to a cutting board, put filling in and roll
  5. Make fish sauce to dip the rice rolls
